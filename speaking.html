Speaking & Appearances

Real stories. Practical systems. Real impact.

Toni speaks on stages, in brokerages, at VR services offices, and wherever people are ready to remove barriers between communities and the real estate market.

Topics

What Toni speaks about

Deaf-Friendly Real Estate

How the real estate industry can better serve the Deaf and hard-of-hearing community — communication strategies, accessibility tools, and why representation matters in every transaction.

Financial Access & Homeownership

Breaking down the barriers that keep families — especially Deaf families — from understanding mortgages, building credit, and confidently entering the housing market.

Entrepreneurship & Systems Building

How to identify gaps in your industry and build scalable, lasting systems that don't just serve clients today — but create generational change in your community.

VR Services & Real Estate Careers

A presentation designed for Vocational Rehabilitation programs: how Deaf individuals can build thriving careers in real estate, and what VR counselors need to know to support them.

Faith, Legacy & Generational Wealth

Blending entrepreneurship, faith, and family — the story of building businesses not just for income, but as a vehicle for long-term impact and legacy for the next generation.

Deaf-Friendly Brokerage Certification

A training track specifically for real estate brokerages — equipping hearing agents to work confidently with Deaf clients and earn certification as a Deaf-friendly professional.

Brokerage Training

Bring Deaf-friendly certification to your team.

The Deaf community is one of the largest underserved populations in real estate. Many agents want to help — they just don't know how.

Toni brings a full training program directly to your brokerage. Your agents leave with practical communication strategies, legal knowledge, and a certification that shows Deaf clients they're in capable hands.

This training is available as a half-day, full-day, or multi-session format — customized to your team's needs.
