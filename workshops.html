Workshops & Training

Hands-on education that changes what's possible.

Two workshop tracks designed for two different audiences — both built around the same belief: that real estate knowledge shouldn't have barriers.

Workshop Tracks

Choose the right track for your audience

For the Deaf Community

Real Estate for Deaf Individuals

An in-person workshop designed specifically for Deaf and hard-of-hearing adults — whether you want to buy or sell your first home, or start a career as a licensed real estate agent.

  • Conducted in ASL with full visual materials
  • Demystify mortgages, contracts, and the closing process
  • Understand your rights as a Deaf buyer or seller
  • Explore what it takes to get your real estate license
  • Ask questions in a safe, judgment-free environment
  • Walk away with a real action plan
Book This Workshop
For Hearing Real Estate Professionals

Deaf-Friendly Agent Certification

A training workshop for licensed agents, brokers, and real estate teams who want to confidently and respectfully serve the Deaf and hard-of-hearing community — and earn certification to prove it.

  • Learn the basics of Deaf culture and communication etiquette
  • Understand interpreter services, legal requirements, and best practices
  • Practical role-play scenarios for showings, offers, and closings
  • Marketing your services to the Deaf community
  • Earn your Deaf-Friendly Agent Certification
  • Available as half-day, full-day, or multi-session
