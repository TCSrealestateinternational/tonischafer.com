About Toni

Lexington, KY — rooted in faith, family, and building for what's next.

I'm Toni Schafer — a Realtor, General Contractor, ASL-fluent educator, and entrepreneur building businesses at the intersection of real estate, accessibility, and generational wealth.

My work blends entrepreneurship, faith, and service — especially through making real estate more accessible to the Deaf community and helping families see that land ownership and financial freedom are genuinely attainable.
